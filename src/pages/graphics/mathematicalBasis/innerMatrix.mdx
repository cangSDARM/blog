<table>
    <tr>
        <th colspan="3" style="text-align: center; font-size:20px;">摄像机及屏幕参数</th>
    </tr>
    <tr>
        <th>_WorldSpaceCameraPos</th>
        <td>float3</td>
        <td>摄像机在世界空间中的位置</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
        <th>_ProjectionParams</th>
        <td>float4</td>
        <td>x=±1, y=Near, z=Far, w=1.0+1.0/Far</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
        <th>_ScreenParams</th>
        <td>float4</td>
        <td>x=width, y=height, z=1.0+1.0/width, w=1.0+1.0/height</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
        <th>_ZBufferParams</th>
        <td>float4</td>
        <td>x=1-Far/Near, y=Far/Near, z=x/Far, w=y/Far, 用于线性化Z缓存的深度值</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
        <th>unity_OrthoParams</th>
        <td>float4</td>
        <td>x=width, y=height, z没有定义, w=1.0(正交)|0.0(透视)</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
        <th>unity_CameraProjection</th>
        <td>float4×4</td>
        <td>摄像机的投影矩阵</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
        <th>unity_CameraInvProjection</th>
        <td>float4×4</td>
        <td>摄像机的投影矩阵的逆矩阵</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
        <th>unity_CameraWorldClipPlanes[6]</th>
        <td>float4</td>
        <td>摄像机的6个裁剪平面在世界空间下的等式, 按: 左右下上近远</td>
    </tr>
</table>
